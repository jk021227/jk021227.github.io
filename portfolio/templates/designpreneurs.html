Introduction

Despite the abundance of habit apps, we still struggle to stay on top of our habits. This pair habit app strives to solutionize this modern struggle with a social touch!

Together, Levith Andrade Cuellar, Pauline Wee, and Jhon Kim set out to create an app that helps people stick to their habits despite busy, hectic schedules and forgetfulness.

Product Overview

After interviewing 15+ people from different backgrounds, we identified that the biggest obstacle was the lack of accountability in being consistent. That's how we came up with Habi.

Habi is an app where you pair up with a partner to complete a habit. You keep each other accountable through a shared, quantifiable pet that you nurture together.
